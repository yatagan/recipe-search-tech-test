Garlic dip
Introduction:
Serve this easy and delectable dip with mixed vegetable crudités – raw bunched carrots and celery, or cooked bunched beetroot. Or try it with beetroot or celeriac crisps. A lovely canapé for summer or winter. 
Ingredients:
2 whole heads dried garlic 1 tbsp olive oil 4 tbsp mayonnaise 4 tbsp whole yoghurt 1 tsp wholegrain mustard salt & pepper
Method:
Preheat oven to 180°C/Gas 4. Brush the garlic heads with the olive oil. Wrap in foil and place in a baking dish in the oven for 25-30 minutes. Remove from the oven and leave to cool slightly. Separate the cloves and peel. Place in a bowl and mash together with a fork. Season with salt to taste. Add the mayonnaise, yoghurt and mustard and mix together. Season with pepper. Chill until required.
