Chickpeas with red pepper
Introduction:
This is a very quick dish to make, and comes mainly from the store cupboard. It will serve 4 as part of a mezze style meal or 2 as an accompaniment to grilled fish or pork steaks. To turn it into a vegetarian main, try tossing with quinoa, crumbled feta and slices of avocado. 
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil 1 small onion, finely chopped 1 red pepper, deseeded & chopped 1 tsp ground cumin small handful fresh coriander, chopped (if none available, use Â½ tsp ground & add with the cumin) 1 tin chickpeas, drained 100ml hot veg stock salt & pepper
Method:
Heat the oil in a pan and gently fry the onion for 5 minutes or so until soft. Add the pepper and cumin, cook for a couple more minutes. Add the chopped fresh coriander, chickpeas and stock. Heat through. Season to taste. Serve warm or cold.
