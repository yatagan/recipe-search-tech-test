Chicken with leeks and honey
Introduction:
This recipes was kindly suggested by a customer as a topping for rice. It also makes a lovely succulent filling for pancakes â€“ use our basic pancake batter. 
Ingredients:
25g butter 2 garlic cloves, peeled & chopped 1 tbsp honey 1 tsp lemon juice 3 tsp soy sauce 1 leek, washed & finely sliced 250g chicken breast, sliced into thin strips salt & pepper
Method:
Fry the butter and add the garlic, honey, lemon juice, soy sauce and 1 tablespoon of water. Let it heat through for a few minutes, then add the leek and chicken and stir the mixture through. Fry until the chicken is done and the leeks cooked (4-5 minutes, depending on the thickness of your chicken strips). Season and serve with rice.
