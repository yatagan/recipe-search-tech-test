Grilled pears in Madeira
Introduction:
This four-ingredient recipe makes a lovely, simple dessert. Serve with cream, ice cream or crème fraîche. 
Ingredients:
2 pears, peeled, cored & quartered juice of ½ lemon 150ml madeira 30g caster sugar
Method:
Sprinkle the pears with lemon juice. In a pan, gently heat the Madeira and sugar until the sugar has dissolved. Increase the heat, add the pears, boil for 2 minutes. Cover and simmer for 5 minutes or until the pears are just tender. Remove from the heat and leave to cool. Once cooled, remove the pears from the syrup and place in a gratin dish. Boil the remaining syrup until reduced by half. Pour the reduced syrup over the pears. Grill for a couple of minutes, until the pears start to brown.
