Raw courgette salad
Introduction:
The UK courgette season is a short one, usually lasting for just July and August. When they are fresh you need do little more than thinly slice and thoughtfully dress them.
Ingredients:
2 courgettes small bunch fresh mint, finely sliced small bunch fresh basil, torn 1 lemon Â½ red chilli, finely chopped salt & pepper
Method:
Using a potato peeler, peel the courgettes into long thin ribbons. Season. Throw together with the mint and basil and add lemon juice and chopped chilli to your taste.
