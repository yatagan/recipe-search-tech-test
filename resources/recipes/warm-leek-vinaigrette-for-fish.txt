Warm leek vinaigrette for fish
Introduction:
This easy way with leeks makes a good topping for grilled white fish or poached chicken. Eat with a bit of fragrant white rice for a clean and healthy lunch or dinner. 
Ingredients:
2 tbsp olive oil 1 large garlic clove, finely sliced 1 leek, cleaned & cut into very fine strips zest & juice of Â½ lemon salt & pepper
Method:
Heat the oil in a frying pan and add the garlic and leek. Fry until both are turning golden. Remove from the heat and add the lemon juice and zest. Season, taste and add a little more lemon juice if you like.
