Potato, celeriac and apple curry
Introduction:
Celeriac and apple are great bedfellows and potato adds a nice heartiness to this quick, one-pot recipe. It makes a good, warming and inexpensive dinner with rice or naan bread. 
Ingredients:
1 tbsp sunflower oil 1 onion, chopped 3 garlic cloves, chopped or crushed 500g celeriac, peeled & cubed 800g potatoes, peeled & cubed 2 small apples, peeled & diced 2 tsp curry paste 1 tsp turmeric 2.5cm fresh ginger, peeled & grated or chopped finely 1 bay leaf 500ml veg stock 50g raisins 4 tbsp yoghurt salt & pepper
Method:
Heat the oil in a pan, add the onion & fry for about 5 minutes until the onion has softened. Add the garlic, celeriac, potatoes & apple. Mix in the curry paste, turmeric, ginger & bay leaf. Add the stock & the raisins. Season to taste and bring to the boil, whilst stirring. Cover & simmer for 15 minutes, continuing to stir at intervals, until the veg is tender. Serve each bowl with a dollop of yoghurt.
