Japanese carrot salad
Introduction:
This quick vegetarian recipe is one to have up your sleeve to serve with any Asian-style meal. It's also good with the addition of grated radishes (or Asian daikon radishes), and some toasted sesame seeds sprinkled on top. 
Ingredients:
3 tbsp rice vinegar 1 tbsp caster sugar a thumb of fresh ginger, peeled & finely grated 500g carrots, coarsely grated salt to taste
Method:
Combine rice vinegar, sugar and ginger. Pour over carrot and add salt to taste.
