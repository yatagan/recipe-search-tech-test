Smoked mackerel, celeriac and watercress salad
Introduction:
Rich smoked mackerel with clean, crunchy celeriac and apple, peppy watercress and fresh herbs. This makes a good healthy lunch or dinner and is robust enough for a packed lunch. If you donâ€™t have watercress, use peppery winter salad leaves instead. You could also add in wedges of cooked beetroot, toasted walnuts or slices or waxy salad potato. 
Ingredients:
1 small celeriac, peeled & cut into fine matchsticks 2 apples, cored & finely sliced squeeze lemon juice 2 tbsp cider vinegar 2 tbsp clear honey 2 tsp coarse grain mustard 4 tbsp olive oil 100g watercress, washed & drained, larger stalks removed small handful fresh herbs, chopped - parsley, tarragon, chives 4 smoked mackerel fillets, shredded (bones removed) salt & pepper
Method:
Toss the celeriac in a large bowl with the apple and lemon juice. Season. In a small bowl, mix the vinegar, honey, mustard and olive oil. Toss into the celeriac with the watercress, herbs and smoked mackerel. Check the seasoning before serving.
