Crispy cod bake
Introduction:
A simple and delicious 30 minute recipe which could be eaten with the traditional mushy peas and tartare sauce, or with broccoli and mash. Try replacing cod with coley from for a more sustainable, marine-life friendly (and cheaper) meal. 
Ingredients:
175g cod fillet 1 tomato, sliced 25g fresh breadcrumbs 15g half fat hard cheese ½ small onion, finely chopped fresh parsley, chopped salt & pepper
Method:
Preheat oven to 190°C/Gas 5. Top the fillet with the sliced tomato and a mixture of the breadcrumbs, cheese, onion and parsley. Season and bake for 20-30 minutes. Serve with vegetables and boiled potatoes.
