ginger and soy marinade
Introduction:

Ingredients:
2 tbsp olive oil 1 tbsp sherry/balsamic vinegar ½ tbsp grated fresh ginger ½ tsp light brown sugar ½ tsp soy sauce
Method:
Mix everything together with the chicken and leave overnight. Cook the chicken, basting with the marinade as you do so. Cooking instructions for chicken Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a frying pan over a medium to high heat. Add the chicken coated in the marinade and fry for 4 mins. Turn and heat for a further 4 minutes until thoroughly cooked and there is no pink meat. To barbecue cook over gentle bbq coals for 4 mins turn and heat for a further 4 minutes until thoroughly cooked, there is no pink meat.
