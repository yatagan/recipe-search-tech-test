Roasted Jerusalem artichokes
Introduction:
Jerusalem artichokes (or earthapples) are pleasingly easily grown. They taste like a sweeter, more subtle version of the potato and can be treated in more of less the same way in the kitchen. These artichokes make a lovely roast dinner side dish. 
Ingredients:
300g Jerusalem artichokes 3 tbsp olive oil few sprigs thyme 2 garlic cloves salt & pepper
Method:
Preheat oven to 200Â°C/Gas 6. Toss Jerusalem artichokes with a the olive oil, spread them out on a baking tray with the thyme, garlic cloves, and some salt and pepper and roast for 30 minutes, until tender.
