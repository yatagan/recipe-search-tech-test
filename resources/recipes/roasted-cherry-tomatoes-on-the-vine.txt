Roasted cherry tomatoes on the vine
Introduction:
Roasted cherry tomatoes caramelise and become lovely and sweet. A sprinkle of sea salt will bring out their sweetness even more. Serve as a side with summer chicken or fish dishes. For a vegetarian meal, this recipe's good with fried halloumi or corn fritters. 
Ingredients:
200g cherry vine tomatoes 2 tbsp olive oil
Method:
Preheat oven to 200Â°C/Gas 6. Drizzle cherry tomatoes with olive oil and roast for about 15 minutes, until their skins are beginning to burst.
