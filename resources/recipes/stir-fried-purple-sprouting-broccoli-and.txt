Stir-fried purple sprouting broccoli and mushrooms
Introduction:
This healthy veggie stir-fry is specially good made with purple sprouting broccoli when it's is season. It can be turned into a vegetarian main by serving it with egg noodles or brown basmati rice. For an optional extra put in 2 tablespoons of sherry when adding the soy sauce. 
Ingredients:
500g purple sprouting broccoli, split into florets, stems sliced 500g mushrooms, finely sliced 250g carrots, cut into matchsticks 1 bunch spring onions, finely sliced 1 garlic clove, crushed 4 tbsp oil 2 tbsp soy sauce 1 tbsp sesame oil
Method:
Stir fry the spring onions and garlic in hot oil for 2 minutes. Add the broccoli and carrot and fry for another 2 minutes. Stir in the mushrooms and fry for 2 minutes then add the soy sauce and cook for a further 2 minutes. Spoon over the sesame oil, season with pepper and remove from the heat. Serve with rice or noodles.
