Masala mussels
Introduction:
This makes a simple and delicious alternative to moules marini√®re: the spiciness of the curry paste is balanced beautifully by cooling, creamy coconut milk. Top with lots of fresh coriander and serve with crusty bread and wedges of lemon. 
Ingredients:
200g masala curry paste 400ml coconut milk 2kg Yealm mussels handful coriander, chopped 2 limes, cut into wedges
Method:
Tip the curry paste and coconut milk into a large saucepan. Mix well and bring to the boil. Add the mussels, cover with a lid and steam for five minutes. shaking occasionally, until they have all opened. Uncover and stir in the coriander. Serve with crusty bread and the wedges of lime.
