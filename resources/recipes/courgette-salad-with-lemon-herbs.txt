Courgette salad with lemon and herbs
Introduction:
Thrown together in five minutes, this healthy, vegetarian side is one for the late summer when courgettes are on overload. Add in a handful of toasted almonds or pine nuts for a bit of crunch. Try this alongside creamy pasta or risotto; in a big bowl at a BBQ; or as a side to grilled fish. 
Ingredients:
zest & juice of ½ lemon 2 tbsp olive oil ½ tbsp mint, chopped ½ tbsp parsley, chopped 4 courgettes, grated 1 tsp poppy seeds salt & pepper
Method:
In a bowl combine the lemon juice and zest, olive oil and herbs. Add the grated courgettes and poppy seeds and toss together to coat the courgettes. Season to taste.
