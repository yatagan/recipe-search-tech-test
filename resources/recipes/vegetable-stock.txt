Vegetable stock
Introduction:
A quick, rich vegetable stock. A decent homemade stock really improves any soup, stew, risotto or sauce. There's no need to be exact with your veg â€“ use up what's left in your fridge. Make a large batch and freeze, ready for use in soups and stews. It will keep for up to a month in the freezer. 
Ingredients:
2 onions, roughly chopped 1 leek, cleaned & roughly chopped 3 celery stalks, chopped 3 carrots, cleaned & roughly chopped 1 garlic clove, crushed or finely chopped 1 bouquet garni a few black peppercorns
Method:
In a large pan, add all the ingredients and bring to the boil. Skim any froth off the top Reduce the heat to a bare simmer, and cook for 30-45 minutes. Remove from the heat, strain through a sieve and leave the liquid to cool.
