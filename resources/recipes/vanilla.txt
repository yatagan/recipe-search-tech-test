Easy ideas for vanilla
Introduction:
Good quality vanilla pods have a delicately pure aroma that's quite different from the artificial vanilla used in many products. These simple ideas are a great way to appreciate the difference. 
Ingredients:
vanilla pods
Method:
Vanilla coffee: Leave a vanilla pod exposed to the air until it goes hard. Break off a small piece and add to your coffee beans in the grinder. Vanilla sugar: Leave a vanilla pod exposed to the air until it goes hard. Grind in a coffee grinder and add to your sugar. Use to flavour tea, coffee, cereal and desserts. You can also keep a few vanilla pods in a jar of sugar and use for baking etc.
