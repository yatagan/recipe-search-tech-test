Simply cooked purple sprouting broccoli, with lemon and butter
Introduction:
A simple and luxuriant recipe for purple sprouting broccoli: a good way to enjoy the first crop of the season. This works as a vegetarian side with pretty much anything, or could be eaten as a starter on its own. 
Ingredients:
500g purple sprouting broccoli, trimmed 50g butter, melted juice & zest of Â½ lemon, more to taste 75g hazelnuts or almonds, toasted & chopped salt & pepper
Method:
Steam or boil broccoli for about 3 minutes, until just tender. Drain, place on a serving platter. Drizzle with the melted butter and lemon juice and zest. Season with salt and pepper and sprinkle over the chopped nuts.
