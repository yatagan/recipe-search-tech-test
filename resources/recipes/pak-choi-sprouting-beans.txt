Pak choi and sprouting beans
Introduction:
This healthy vegetarian stir-fry makes a great quick dinner with a bowl of fluffy jasmine rice. Pak choi (or bok choi) can be boiled, steamed, saut√©ed or added to stir-fries, soups or salads. 
Ingredients:
1 tbsp sesame oil 3 spring onions, sliced 2 garlic cloves, cut in slivers 2cm piece fresh ginger 1 carrot, cut into matchsticks 125g sprouting beans 1 pak choi, shredded 50g unsalted cashew nuts 2 tbsp dry sherry 3 tbsp light soy sauce 1 tsp cornflour 1 tsp honey 35ml cold water salt & pepper
Method:
Heat the oil in a large wok and fry the onions, garlic, ginger and carrot for 2 minutes. Add the sprouting beans and fry for another 2 minutes. Add the pak choi and nuts and stir fry until the leaves are just wilting. Quickly mix all the remaining ingredients in a jug and pour into the wok. Stir to coat the vegetables in the sauce, season and serve immediately.
