Involtini di peperoni
Introduction:
Bursting with Mediterranean flavours, these peppers make a delicious starter or tapas with drinks. They're good both warm and cold. To keep them vegetarian substitute the Parmesan for another hard cheese. 
Ingredients:
3 ripe tomatoes 300g mozzarella, cut into cubes 4 red peppers 10 black olives, stoned & chopped 2 tbsp capers, rinsed & chopped 2 tbsp Parmesan, grated 2 tbsp breadcrumbs, toasted 2 garlic cloves, crushed handful basil, shredded 1 tbsp parsley, chopped olive oil salt & pepper
Method:
Preheat oven to 220°C/Gas 7. Blanch the tomatoes in boiling water, remove the skin, core and seeds. Chop the tomatoes and mix with mozzarella. Place the peppers in the oven until the skin blisters. Transfer to a bowl, cover with cling film and leave to cool. Next, peel the peppers, remove seeds and cut in half. Reserve the liquid in the bowl. Reduce oven temperature to 190°C/Gas 5. Add the tomatoes, mozzarella, olives, capers, Parmesan, breadcrumbs and garlic to a bowl. Season and mix well. Divide mixture between the peppers. Sprinkle with basil and parsley, and drizzle with the pepper liquid and olive oil. Bake in the oven for 15 minutes.
