Kachumber salad
Introduction:
This unfussy, punchy vegan side salad is popularly eaten in India alongside spicy dishes. It's also great on it's own as a healthy snack. It has many variations - you could grate instead of dicing, or add any of cucumber, carrots and beetroot. Other common additions are mint leaves and minced fresh chilli. 
Ingredients:
200g tomatoes, diced 1 medium onion, diced 4 tbsp coriander, or parsley or mint, chopped ½ tsp cumin, roasted & ground ½ tsp cayenne pepper 2 tbsp lemon juice or vinegar ¾ tsp salt
Method:
Combine all the ingredients together in a bowl.
