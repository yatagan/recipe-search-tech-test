Curried cabbage and apple coleslaw
Introduction:
Very easy to make, this is a good recipe for a barbecue, picnic or light lunch. It has a nice kick from the curry powder, layered with sweetness of the raisins and apples and crunch from the cabbage and cashews. 
Ingredients:
½ medium pointed cabbage, or spring or summer greens, finely shredded 2 apples, cored & thinly sliced 2 spring or 1 bunched onion, finely sliced (use white & green parts) 2 tbsp crème fraîche or mayonnaise 2 tbsp plain yoghurt 1 tsp medium curry powder 2 tbsp raisins 50g cashew nuts, toasted in a dry frying pan for a couple of mins until golden, roughly chopped salt & pepper
Method:
Combine all the ingredients in a large bowl, serve.
