Carrots cooked in honey and ginger
Introduction:
Ginger adds a twist to a favourite that's popular with all ages. This makes an easy dinner side that could be eaten alongside brown rice and chicken. 
Ingredients:
500g carrots, very thinly sliced 1 tbsp butter 1 tbsp honey juice of 1 lemon 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
Method:
Put the carrots in a wide pan with the butter, honey, lemon and ginger. Cover and cook slowly until tender.
