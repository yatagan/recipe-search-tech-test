Thai chicken curry
Introduction:
This fragrant 20-minute Thai chicken curry makes a delectable midweek dinner. If you have more time, try making your own red Thai curry paste â€“ you'll be astonished at the difference. Eat it with boiled or steamed white rice, and a good handful of coriander if you have some around. 
Ingredients:
300g diced chicken 2 tbsp red Thai curry paste 75ml coconut milk 75ml veg stock 1-2 tbsp palm or light brown sugar 1-2 tbsp fish sauce fresh coriander, chopped (optional) cashew nuts, toasted (optional
Method:
Mix the diced chicken with the curry paste and leave to marinate for an hour. In a hot pan, quickly fry the chicken for 3-4 minutes, then add the coconut milk, veg stock, sugar and fish sauce. Bring to a simmer and cook gently for 15 minutes. The whole dish can be lifted by a garnish or sprinkling of chopped coriander leaves and toasted cashew nuts.
