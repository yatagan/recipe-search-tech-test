Middle Eastern lamb marinade
Introduction:
A beautiful marinade for an alternative Sunday roast. Smear over a lamb roasting joint on Saturday night and leave to marinade overnight. 
Ingredients:
small bunch fresh mint small bunch fresh coriander 1 orange, zested & juiced 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped ¼ tsp each smoked paprika, cinnamon, cumin & ground coriander ¼ tsp salt ¼ tsp allspice olive oil
Method:
Blitz everything together with enough olive oil to make a paste. Use as a marinade for an alternative Sunday lamb roast.
