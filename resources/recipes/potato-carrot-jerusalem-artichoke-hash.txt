Potato, carrot and Jerusalem artichoke hash
Introduction:
This simple boiled-then-baked vegetarian hash is meal to warm your bones. It's a great dinner for using up the odd veg left in your box â€“ there's no need to be too exact with the ingredients. It particularly nice topped off with a fried egg. 
Ingredients:
2 Jerusalem artichokes, scrubbed or peeled & chopped 3 carrots, peeled & chopped 1 onion, chopped 3 potatoes, peeled & chopped 15g butter, cubed into small pieces 250ml veg stock salt & pepper
Method:
Put the veg into a pan with the stock and bring to the boil. Simmer for 20-30 minutes. Remove from the heat, pour off the excess liquid. Add seasoning to taste. Mash the veg to form a smooth paste. Transfer to a dish and smooth the top. Dot with butter, bake in a hot oven for 10-15 minutes until golden brown.
