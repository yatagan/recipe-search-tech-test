Chinese plum sauce
Introduction:
The sauce is a great complement not only to Chinese duck dishes, but also to roast chicken and pork, and as a a dip for spring rolls. It's easy to make and will keep well in the fridge for up to two weeks. 
Ingredients:
450g plums, halved 2 red chillies, deseeded 150g caster sugar 100ml white wine vinegar 150ml cold water 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated (optional) salt & pepper
Method:
Place all of the ingredients in a heavy based pan. Cover and simmer gently for about 20 minutes until the plums are soft and collapsed. Rub through a sieve to remove stones and skins. Taste and adjust seasonings.
