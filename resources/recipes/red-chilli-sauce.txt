Red chilli sauce
Introduction:
This easy to make chilli sauce is a good addition to practically anything (if you like things hot!). Try it with scallops, squid, fish or lamb, or drizzled over quesadillas. 
Ingredients:
2 red chillies, deseeded & chopped 1 garlic clove, crushed 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped 2 tbsp olive oil
Method:
Mix all the ingredients together.
