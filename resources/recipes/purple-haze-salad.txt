Purple haze salad
Introduction:
Take advantage of your colourful carrots with this simple side. 
Ingredients:
2-3 large purple carrots 1 lemon pinch ground cardamom pinch ground ginger pinch ground cinnamon 50g feta or goatâ€™s cheese 30g pine nuts, lightly toasted small bunch flat leaf parsley, roughly chopped salt & pepper
Method:
Wash and peel the carrots, use the peeler to peel the carrots into long ribbon like strips. Throw the strips in a bowl and season with salt, pepper and a generous squeeze of lemon juice. Add the spices, mix well and adjust the seasoning to your taste. Pile the carrots artfully on a plate. Crumble over the cheese and scatter with the pine nuts and parsley.
