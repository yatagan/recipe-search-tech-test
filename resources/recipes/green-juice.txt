Green juice
Introduction:
This simple green juice is full of green goodness. Adapt it to your taste and veg box – spinach works in place of kale, add an apple if you'd like it a little sweeter, or minced ginger for more of a kick. 
Ingredients:
150g curly kale ½ cucumber ½ broccoli head (stalk & florets) squeeze of lemon juice, to taste
Method:
Juice the kale, cucumber and broccoli, then squeeze in a little lemon juice to taste.
