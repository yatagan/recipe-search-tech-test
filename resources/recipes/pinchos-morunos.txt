Pinchos morunos
Introduction:
Mojo picón, is a sauce/relish from the Canary Isles, made from red peppers, stale bread and chillies. With this in mind, the latest go-to diced chicken recipe has to be moreish, Moorish pinchos morunos. 
Ingredients:
500g diced chicken for the marinade: 1 tsp smoked sweet paprika 1 tsp cumin 1 tsp dried oregano 1 sprig fresh thyme 1 garlic clove 3 tbsp olive oil
Method:
Soak 10 small wooden skewers in water for 30 minutes. Blitz the marinade ingredients together and mix with the diced chicken in a bowl. Skewer your meat – don’t push it too tightly together or it won’t cook properly. Brush with olive oil and fry, grill or BBQ to your liking, served with mojo picón sauce.
