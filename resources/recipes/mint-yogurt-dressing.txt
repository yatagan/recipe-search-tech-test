Mint & yoghurt dressing
Introduction:
Mix with chopped cucumber, apple and celery, spoon over a warm tray of spicy roasted baby carrots or dollop into pitta bread with roasted lamb and salad leaves.
Ingredients:
1 tbsp red wine vinegar 3 tbsp yoghurt 1 tbsp olive oil 4-5 large mint leaves, chopped salt & pepper
Method:
Pop everything in a jam jar and shake like Elvis.
