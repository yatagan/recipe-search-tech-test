Pork with white beans, buttered braised cabbage and caraway seeds
Introduction:
Pork and cabbage are a classic combination in cooking the world over – add a tin of beans and you have a complete and delicious 20 minute meal. 
Ingredients:
small knob butter 1 tsp caraway seeds ½ large January King cabbage, shredded oil for frying, e.g. vegetable 500g pork steaks 250ml hot chicken or veg stock 1 tin cannellini beans, drained & rinsed a few fresh thyme leaves, or ½ tsp dried salt & pepper
Method:
Melt a little butter in a pan. Add the caraway seeds and fry for a minute or two. Add the cabbage, cover and cook for about 10 minutes, until the cabbage is soft. Season with salt and pepper. Meanwhile add a little oil or half butter and half oil to a large frying pan and cook the pork until almost cooked through. Add the stock, cannellini beans and thyme. Season with salt and pepper. Continue to simmer for another 5 minutes or until the pork is cooked through. Serve on top of the cabbage.
