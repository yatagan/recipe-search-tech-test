Kale and olive pasta
Introduction:
This nutrient-rich recipe has a lovely rich flavour profile and can be thrown together in twenty minutes. Substitute the Parmesan if you are vegetarian, and use gluten-free pasta if you'd prefer. 
Ingredients:
250g dried pasta 150g kale, washed & stalks removed 100g butter 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped 25g walnut pieces, roughly chopped 25g pitted black olives, roughly chopped 2 tsp balsamic vinegar Parmesan, grated salt & pepper
Method:
Cook the pasta as per the pack instructions. Shred the kale leaves and blanch in boiling water for 2-3 minutes. Melt the butter in a large frying pan or wok. Add the garlic, walnuts and olives and cook for 2-3 minutes. Add the kale, the drained pasta, balsamic vinegar and salt and pepper. Cook for a further 1-2 minutes. Sprinkle with Parmesan and serve.
