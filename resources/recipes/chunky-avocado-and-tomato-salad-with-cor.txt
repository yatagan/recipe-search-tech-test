Chunky avocado and tomato salad with coriander and sweet chilli dressing
Introduction:
The key to this lovely summer salad is ripe tomatoes and avocados – don't try and make it without. Serve this as an accompaniment to chilli con carne or marinated fish, or just on its own for a light vegetarian lunch. 
Ingredients:
2-3 ripe avocados 500g tomatoes ¼ cucumber ½ red onion or 1 bunch of spring onions, chopped 1 garlic clove, crushed 1 tbsp sweet chilli sauce 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar 2 tbsp sunflower oil 2 tbsp coriander, chopped juice of ½ lemon or lime salt & pepper
Method:
Cut the avocados, tomatoes and cucumber into chunky 2cm dice. Mix together in a bowl with the onion and season well. Mix all the remaining ingredients together and season well. Toss with the vegetables and serve.
