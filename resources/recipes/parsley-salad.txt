Parsley salad
Introduction:
This is almost a salsa rather than a salad. It's great with grilled fish, chicken or lamb. To enjoy it in vegetarian form, try it on crostini, or mixed with orzo pasta. 
Ingredients:
leaves from large bunch flat-leaf parsley 1 garlic clove, crushed with a pinch of salt 8 anchovies, chopped 1 tbsp capers, soaked in water for 20 mins, then drained & squeezed dry 8 sun-dried tomatoes, chopped 1 red onion, chopped 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar 3 tbsp olive oil
Method:
Put all the ingredients in a bowl and toss together well.
