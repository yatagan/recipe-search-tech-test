Beef merguez
Introduction:
You could serve these as little meatballs with a lamb shawarma. 
Ingredients:
500g beef mince 3 tbsp breadcrumbs 1 generous tbsp herby harissa paste Â½ tsp salt
Method:
Mix all the ingredients together in a mixing bowl. Knead until it just begins to come together. Form into patties, meatballs or, if you have some kebab sticks, sausage shapes. Griddle, grill or barbecue until cooked through.
