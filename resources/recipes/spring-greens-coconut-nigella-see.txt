Summer greens with coconut and nigella seeds
Introduction:
A simple subcontinent side. Perfect with a curry or dahl. 
Ingredients:
250g spring or summer greens 1 tbsp coconut or plain oil 2 tbsp desiccated coconut 1 tsp nigella (black onion seeds)
Method:
Strip the leaves from the stalks and wash them. Roll them together and slice them into very fine shreds. Heat the oil in a deep pan, add the greens, coconut, nigella seeds and a pinch of salt. Cook on a medium heat for 5 minutes until wilted and tender, add a dash of water if they start to dry out. Add more salt & pepper to taste.
