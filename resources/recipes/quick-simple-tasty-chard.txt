Quick and simple tasty chard
Introduction:
Add punchy ginger and garlic, hot chilli and salty soy sauce for a flavour-packed, quick and easy way with chard. This also works with other greens â€“ spinach, kale, spring and winter greens and cabbage. It makes a good vegetarian and vegan side to pretty much anything. 
Ingredients:
chard garlic ginger chilli soy sauce
Method:
As the white stalks and green leaves cook at different rates, the best way to cook chard it is to chop them up separately, then stir fry just the stalks in a little oil for a few minutes, until almost tender. Add the leaves with a little chopped garlic, ginger and chilli, then fry for a couple more minutes or so, adding a little soy sauce to finish.
