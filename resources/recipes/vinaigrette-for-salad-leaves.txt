Vinaigrette for salad leaves
Introduction:
Knowledge of how to make a basic vinaigrette is a must in any vaguely foodie household. There are countless variations - the addition of garlic, lemon juice, herbs, flavoured oils and vinegars etc. - but this is a great springboard recipe for further experimentation.
Ingredients:
225ml olive oil 75ml vinegar (red, white, balsamic or cider) 1 tbsp Dijon mustard salt & pepper
Method:
Whisk together all the ingredients, or shake in a jam jar. Keep in the fridge, for up to a week or two.
