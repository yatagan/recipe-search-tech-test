Vanilla custard
Introduction:
This easy to make vanilla custard can be poured over traditional sponge puddings, tarts, cakes or ice creams, or layered in a trifle. It's also a sumptuous dessert eaten on its own in a bowl with fresh strawberries or raspberries piled on top. 
Ingredients:
300ml single cream 1 vanilla pod (seeds only) 4 egg yolks 1 tsp sugar 1 tsp cornflour
Method:
Heat the cream and vanilla gently in a pan. In a bowl, mix the eggs yolks with the sugar and cornflour. When the cream comes to the boil, pour over the egg yolk mixture and mix together well.
