Simple shallot salad
Introduction:
A fast and simple salad with a raw punch and crunch. It is often said that parsley can be chewed to combat onion or garlic breath; here you have the cause and the cure in one dish. Great with pickled or smoked fish or as a side to any hot dish. 
Ingredients:
handful fresh parsley, chopped small sprig mint, chopped 4 shallots, finely sliced 1 tbsp rinsed capers, chopped zest & juice of 1 lemon salt
Method:
Throw the herbs, onions and capers together with a tablespoon of olive oil and a pinch of salt. Add a sprinkle of zest and a splash of lemon juice, adding more as your taste decides.
