Steamed scallops with ginger
Introduction:
These classic oriental ingredients add a delicate flavour to simple steamed scallops and make an elegant dinner party starter. Scallops are in season in the UK from late July to December. 
Ingredients:
16 scallops 2cm fresh ginger, peeled & finely chopped 2 spring onions, finely sliced 100ml sesame oil 2 tbsp soy sauce 25g fresh coriander, roughly chopped
Method:
Sprinkle the scallops with the ginger and spring onions, and steam gently for about 5 minutes. Warm through the sesame oil and soy sauce. Pour over the scallops, sprinkle the chopped coriander over the top and serve.
