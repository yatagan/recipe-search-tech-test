Spinach with yoghurt
Introduction:
Nutritious, simple and quick to cook. The combination of earthy spinach and cooling yoghurt is lovely with curry and dal, or as part of a mezze spread. 
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped 2 tbsp sunflower oil 225g cooked spinach, roughly chopped 600ml Greek-style yoghurt 1 garlic clove, crushed powdered sumac or sweet paprika dried mint, to garnish salt & pepper
Method:
Saut√© the onion in the oil until browned. Cool slightly. Mix with the spinach, yoghurt, garlic and seasoning. Spoon into a serving bowl and chill until nearly ready to serve. Decorate the surface with a scattering of sumac or sweet paprika, and dried mint.
