Egyptian toasted sandwich
Introduction:
If you’ve had enough burgers, ragus and chillies, try a kind of Egyptian toasted sandwich or Hawawshi. 
Ingredients:
300g mince 1 garlic clove, diced 1 onion, diced 2 tomatoes, diced 2 red peppers, seeded & diced 1 tsp fresh mint, chopped 1 tsp fresh parsley, chopped ½ tsp chilli, chopped pinch each ground cumin, coriander & cloves 4 fresh pitta breads olive oil pinch za'atar, dukkah or ras-el-hanout
Method:
Preheat oven to 200°C/Gas 6. Put your mince in a food processor with diced garlic, onion, tomato and peppers. Add the mint, chilli and parsley and a good pinch each of ground cumin, coriander and cloves. Pulse a couple of times. Spoon the filling into the breads, pinch shut, brush with oil on both sides and sprinkle with zaatar, dukkah or ras-el-hanout. Wrap in foil/parchment and bake for 15 minutes, turn over and cook for another 10 minutes.
