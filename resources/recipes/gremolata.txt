Gremolata
Introduction:
Sprinkle gremolata over slow-roasted meat or veg; over chicken or fish; or use to embellish pasta, soups or stews, or even as a pizza topping. 
Ingredients:
4 tbsp parsley, chopped 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped zest of 1 lemon
Method:
Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl.
