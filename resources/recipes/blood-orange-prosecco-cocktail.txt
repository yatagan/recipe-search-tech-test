Blood orange and Prosecco cocktail
Introduction:
A celebratory drink for early in the year when blood oranges are in season – just in time for Valentine's Day. Use prosecco or if you're feeling extravagant, champagne. A splash of Campari doesn't hurt either. 
Ingredients:
juice of 1-2 blood oranges prosecco or Champagne
Method:
Fill a champagne flute or wine glass ⅓ full with freshly squeezed blood orange juice. Top up with prosecco.
