Lemon onion sauce
Introduction:
This easy, zesty sauce goes very well with meaty grilled fish or chicken, and can also be used to dress a plate of veg. 
Ingredients:
450g onions, chopped 25g butter olive oil pared zest & juice of 1 lemon salt & pepper
Method:
