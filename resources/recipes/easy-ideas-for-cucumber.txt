Easy ideas for cucumber
Introduction:
Delicate, buttery saut√©ed cucumbers go well with anything from a curry to barbecued meat and grilled white fish. 
Ingredients:
1 cucumber 1 tbsp butter lemon juice chopped herbs such as dill & parsley
Method:
Peel a cucumber, quarter it lengthways and remove the seeds. Chop the flesh into 2cm chunks. Melt the butter in a pan and toss the cucumber in it, stirring well. Cook gently for 5 minutes, then add lemon juice, salt and pepper to taste. Stir in the chopped herbs. Serve with fish.
