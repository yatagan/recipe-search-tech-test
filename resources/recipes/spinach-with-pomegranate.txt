Spinach with pomegranate
Introduction:
This dish has a beautiful, gem-studded appearance. A good way to cook the spinach is to saut√© it with a little minced garlic, olive oil, salt and pepper. Serve as part of a mezze or with grilled fish. 
Ingredients:
400g spinach seeds & juice of 1 pomegranate
Method:
Blanch the spinach in boiling water for 2 minutes. Drain, refresh and then squeeze out any excess moisture. Leave to cool. Dress cold spinach with pomegranate juice and seeds.
